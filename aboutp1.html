Who we are?

We are a team who tries to provide a platform to peoples who are looking for investor or man power or service provider or business partner etc, anything related to his/her own business. We are making a community for business/entreprenurs associations.




About Us

This community was founded in March, 2022 by FF. This is an independent organization with its own Board of Trustees.



Vision:

BUSINESS TALKINGS envisions promoting professionals and excellent associations between businessmans by the technological progress and development needs of the country.



Mission:

Business Talkings is committed to provide quality and excellent services responsive to the emerging challenges of the time. It is dedicated to produce competent world class professional business association with strong ethical values.



Goals:

*Sustain development and progress of the businesses of the county.

*Continue to upgrade services and facilities responsive of the demands for change and needs of the society

*Inculcate professional culture among management, faculty and personnel in the attainment of the institution's vision, mission and goals.

*Establish strong networking of programs, sharing of resources and expertise with local and international institutions and organizations.
